What we do

Using an adjustable machine-learning algorithm, our program analyzes any set of data to create linkages that can then be conveyed to our partner organizations.

Data Ingestion

With the appropriate file(s), we feed in data into our program.

Tuning the Algorithm

Our algorithm is then fine tuned by adjusting various parameters to meet the requirements of the test.

Data Verification

All of the data is confirmed to be suitable for analysis prior to testing.

Finding Patterns

The test is run and analyzed for significant connections between varying sets of data.

Building Connections

These obtained results are run through our vast database of organizations to create the appropriate connections.

Final Result

With some human touches, we prepare for a formal consultation with our client and discuss the path forward.

Our Work

Addressing Illiteracy In the USA

When addressing how to combat the issue of suicide around the world, most would not likely look towards the energy sector for a solution, but that’s exactly where we went. By analyzing data provided by the World Bank regarding suicides in the Central African Republic, we found that greater access to electricity among the population had a strong correlation with the recent reduction of suicides in the country. While seemingly unorthodox, these results allow us to formulate a relationship between suicide prevention and non-profits who seek to improve access to this vital resource in surrounding areas and nations in order to further mitigate similar issues.
